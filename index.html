About Me

I am a Cyber Security Analyst with a strong background in SOC operations, incident analysis and security monitoring, combined with a multidisciplinary creative and entrepreneurial mindset.

I am also the founder of two independent creative ventures: one dedicated to visual art and digital expression, and another focused on natural, ritual-based wellness products. I believe in combining technology, creativity and education to build meaningful, ethical and sustainable projects.
